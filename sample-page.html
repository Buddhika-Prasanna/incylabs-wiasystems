When selecting software, it’s all about having the ability to choose the solutions that work best for your business. Not only in terms of functionality, pricing and deployment options are equally important in ensuring a good fit with a business’ needs. Availability of skilled staff, capital, hardware and networking infrastructure all play a role in choosing licensing and deployments models. No matter what your business’ priorities are IFS’s cloud solutions allow you to run IFS enterprise software your way. You choose where it’s run, how it’s maintained and how you pay to license and use it. With IFS cloud solutions for IFS Applications and IFS Field Service Management you will be able to embrace the benefits of moving your business-critical IT assets to the cloud.
